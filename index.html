Lets connect

Product designer based in Oakland, California currently working for Google as a Sr. Staff Designer, leading and managing a team of designers responsible for Android Automotive OS.


Since joining Google in 2011 I have had the opportunity to work on incredible projects small and big. From designing Google Doodles, helping startups with Google Ventures to building new products for Shopping Express Mobile as well as leading major products and the vision for Google Search, Google Pay, and the games efforts of Google Play.


I am also the founder of Abduzeedo, an award-winning digital publication about design and a personal project that has become the source of inspiration for millions of designers and enthusiasts, and Calmaria, an application created to help people with anxiety through a very simple breathing exercise.
