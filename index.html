Lets connect

As a Senior Staff Designer based in Oakland, California, I'm currently working at Google and leading a team of designers in charge of the Android Automotive Operating System, Google Automotive Services (GAS) and Android Auto phone projection product.

Since joining the Big G in 2011, I've had the opportunity to work on some pretty wild projects - from designing Google Doodles to helping startups through Google Ventures, to building new products for Shopping Express Mobile. I've also been lucky enough to lead the vision for major products like Google Search, Google Pay, Google Shopping, Google Play Music, and Google Play's gaming efforts.

In my free time (haha, what's that?), I'm the mastermind behind Abduzeedo, an award-winning digital publication all about design. It started as a personal project, but has now become a source of inspiration for millions of designers and enthusiasts around the world. And if that wasn't enough, I also created Calmaria, an app that helps people find a little peace of mind through a simple breathing exercise. It's like therapy, but cheaper!
