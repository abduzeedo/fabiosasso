Calmaria in Portuguese means calmness, tranquility, calm. It’s often used by seamen and surfers to indicate that there are no waves in the ocean. That mood perfectly translates to what this app intends to do, bring peace of mind and tranquility to your busy day to day life. Just breathe and relax.

Terral is Portuguese for the offshore breeze that originates in mountain woods. Intended for use with our breathing app, Calmaria, Terral is a gender-neutral fragrance, flexible for layering or use on its own. For anyone needing stress-relief and tranquility, apply Terral lightly to pulse points as a gentle reminder to breathe in and take time for yourself.
