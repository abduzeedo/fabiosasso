About me

As a product designer, I'm all about putting the user first in my work. I have a deep understanding of user-centered design principles and I'm passionate about creating intuitive and engaging user experiences aligned with product strategy and business needs.

My experience working with large teams and on complex projects has given me the skills and expertise I need to see projects through from start to finish, making sure the end result is a top-quality product that meets the needs of users.

I'm always looking to stay current with the latest design trends and technologies. I believe it's important to stay up to date in this field, and I'm always on the lookout for new challenges and opportunities to push the boundaries of UX design. Whether I'm working on a new project or just learning about the latest techniques, I'm always striving to be the best designer I can be.

Calmaria

I am the creator of Calmaria, a breathing app designed to bring peace of mind and tranquility to your daily life. The word "calmaria" is a Portuguese term meaning calmness, tranquility, and calm. It is often used by seamen and surfers to indicate that there are no waves in the ocean. This concept perfectly translates to the intention of my app - to help you relax and find tranquility amidst the chaos of daily life. Simply take a few deep breaths and let Calmaria guide you towards a more peaceful state of mind. If you are interested in learning more, please visit calmaria.app
